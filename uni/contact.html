Getting to Campus

By Car:

Take Highway 101 to Exit 25, then follow University Avenue for 2 miles. Campus parking is available in designated lots.

By Public Transit:

Take Metro Bus Route 42 or 55 to University Station. The campus is a 5-minute walk from the station.

By Air:

Fly into Education City International Airport (ECI), then take a taxi or shuttle service to campus (approximately 20 minutes).
