About Oakwood University

Discover our history, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 1970, Oakwood University has been at the forefront of higher education for over five decades. What began as a small liberal arts college has grown into a comprehensive university serving students from around the world.

Our commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and student success has made us a leading institution in higher education. We pride ourselves on fostering an environment where students can explore their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and prepare for successful careers.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to achieve their full potential, contribute to society, and become leaders in their chosen fields through innovative teaching, research, and service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier university that transforms lives through education, advances knowledge through research, and serves as a catalyst for positive change in our communities and beyond.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from teaching and research to student services and campus life.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards and promote honesty, trust, and respect in all our interactions.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to enhance learning and advance knowledge.

Compassion

We care deeply about our students, colleagues, and communities, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment.
