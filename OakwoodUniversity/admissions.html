Admissions

Start your journey to academic excellence

Welcome to Oakwood University

We're excited that you're considering Oakwood University for your higher education journey. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic environment and contribute to our vibrant campus community.

Whether you're a high school senior, transfer student, or graduate school applicant, we're here to guide you through every step of the application process.

Quick Facts

Acceptance Rate: 65%
Average GPA: 3.5

Undergraduate Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • 4 years of English
  • 3 years of Mathematics
  • 3 years of Science
  • 3 years of Social Studies

Test Scores

  • SAT: Minimum 1200 (Evidence-Based Reading and Writing + Math)
  • ACT: Minimum 25 composite score
  • Test-optional policy available

Supporting Documents

  • Official high school transcripts
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Resume of activities and achievements

Graduate Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Prerequisites for specific programs
  • Relevant coursework in field of study

Test Scores

  • GRE: Minimum 310 (Verbal + Quantitative)
  • GMAT: Minimum 550 (for business programs)
  • Program-specific requirements may apply

Supporting Documents

  • Official college transcripts
  • Statement of purpose (750-1000 words)
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Research proposal (for research programs)

International Student Requirements

English Proficiency

  • TOEFL iBT: Minimum 80
  • IELTS: Minimum 6.5
  • Duolingo: Minimum 110
  • Cambridge English: Minimum 180

Academic Credentials

  • Credential evaluation by approved agency
  • Official transcripts (translated to English)
  • Certificates and diplomas
  • Grade conversion documentation

Financial & Visa

  • Bank statements showing adequate funds
  • Affidavit of support
  • Passport copy
  • I-20 form processing
